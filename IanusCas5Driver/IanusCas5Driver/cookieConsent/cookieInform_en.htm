This page offers information on the use and management of cookies and similar technologies.

Definitions

A cookie is a small piece of data (letters and/or numbers) that allows a webserver to store information on the client (browser) to be utilized during the visit only (session cookies) or for a pre-defined period of time (persistent cookies).
The cookie allows the website to "remember" users’ actions or preferences over time on their computers or mobile devices.
Similar technologies such as web beacon, transparent GIFs and all forms of local storage introduced with HTML5 can be used to collect information on users behaviours and use of services.
In the following text we will use the words ‘cookie’ or ‘cookies’ to refer to cookies and all similar technologies.
